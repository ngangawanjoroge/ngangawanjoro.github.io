Input/Output


Thank You for your support. Incase this is your first use these notes may be of help;
  1. In the basic mathematics i.e (+,-,/,*) write the question as it is eg 5+7 then press the = Button.
  2. In finding square (x2) ,square root (✓) and the trigonometric ratios write the number first then press the button accordingly.
  3. The AC button clears the output.
  4. The DEl button deletes text or numbers one at a time.
  5. For Quadratic Equations(Quad button) press the button and you are requested for numbers in (a,b,c,d) order. Equation format(ax2+bx+c=0)
  6. For Simultaneous Equations(Simult Button) press the button and you are requested for numbers in order(a,b,c,d,e,f) order. The order is very important. Example Equations 
                            ax+by=c
                        dx+ey=f
    It may take sometime according to the complexity of the equations.
  7. Values that are left blank are treated as Zeros(0).
